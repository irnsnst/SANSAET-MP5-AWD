It is made with Poplar body paired with maple neck and Laurel fretboard - a similar tonewood to rosewood, only with a darker shade. The components and hardware of the S-3 remain the same with a vintage 6-point tremolo with bent steel saddle for a vintage bite, combined with a big steel block for increased sustain.
